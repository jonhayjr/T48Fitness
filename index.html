About

T48 Fitness was found by Jon and Sherry Hay in 2025. Their goal was to create a fitness that not only focused on physical health but on spiritual health as well. At the end of the day, they believe that spiritual training matters most.

For physical training is of some value, but godliness has value for all things, holding promise for both the present life and the life to come. -1 Timothy 4:8
