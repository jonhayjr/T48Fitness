About

T48 Fitness was founded by Jon and Sherry Hay in 2025. Their goal was to create a fitness that not only focused on physical health but on spiritual health as well. At the end of the day, they believe that spiritual training matters most.

For physical training is of some value, but godliness has value for all things, holding promise for both the present life and the life to come. -1 Timothy 4:8

Services

Resistance

We use a combination of dumbells and resistance bands to help you shape your body.

Cardio

Cardio doesn't have to be boring. We'll create a custom cardio plan that is fun and maintainable.

Nutrition

We'll create a custom nutrition plan that allows you to still eat the foods that you love.

Godliness

What sets us apart from our competitors is our focus on spiritual health. We want to help you become a man or woman after God's own heart.
